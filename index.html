Favourite Place - Tirumala


Lord Venkateswara Temple at Tirumala (Tirupati) is one of the most famous temples in India. With more than 35 million pilgrims visiting the temple every year, it is one of the most visited holy places and also one of the richest temples in the world. Built on the Venkata Hill, which is a part of the famous Seshachalam Hills, Tirupati Temple is considered as one of the holiest shrines by the Hindus. According to a legend, Lord Vishnu assumed the form of Sri Venkateswara to save mankind from the adversities of Kali Yuga.



Favourite Food - Chicken Biryani


Chicken biryani is one of India's most popular dishes, and it is believed that the Mughals were the first to introduce this delicious and flavorful dish in this country. The reason why chicken biryani is so famous is due to the different flavours and the ingredients it carries as well as the nostalgia it evokes as a royal and iconic dish. One of the most common ways to prepare chicken biryani is to layer spiced chicken followed by rice, fried onions, and ghee.

Chicken biryani is so popular across the country that different regions have their own unique style of making the biryani. With its huge popularity it is considered to be a charming treat that people love to enjoy on special occasions. There are several popular versions of Indian chicken biryani that are named after the regions in which it was developed.



Favourite Hero - Sundar Pichai


Sundar Pichai was born in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India, on June 10, 1972. Pichai's full name is Pichai Sundararajan. He is a prominent business leader best known as Google Inc.'s Chief Executive Officer (CEO) and also the CEO of Alphabet Inc., the firm's parent corporation. Pichai has received widespread acclaim for his leadership, abilities, and product vision. He is credited with popularizing the Android operating system.

Pichai showed an early interest in technology and an exceptional recall, particularly for phone digits. After graduating from the Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur with a degree in metallurgy (B.Tech,1993) and a silver medal (M.S.), he was awarded a scholarship to Stanford University. He stayed in the United States after that, temporarily working for Applied Materials (a supplier of semiconductor materials) before receiving an M.B.A. (2002) from the University of Pennsylvania's Wharton School. He is married to Anjali Pichai, a graduate of Kasturba Medical College in Manipal and a native of Vellore, Tamil Nadu.



Favourite Herione - Mangli


Satyavathi Rathod, colloquially and popularly known as Mangli, is an Indian playback singer, television presenter and actress. She is popular for her traditional Banjara attire and known for her Telangana songs and performance at festival events in India and abroad. She is presently working with a Telugu-language web channel, Mic TV, featuring in popular video songs on special occasions like Bathukamma, Bonalu, Sankranthi, Telangana Formation Day, Ugadhi, Sammakka Sarakka Jatara and many.



Favourite Actor - Suriya


Suriya was born to actor Sivakumar and his wife Lakshmi in Chennai, but brought up in Coimbatore. He is the eldest of three children; his younger brother is actor Karthi Sivakumar and he has a younger sister named Brindha Sivakumar. He attended PSBB School and St. Bede's School in Chennai and graduated from Loyola College with a Bachelor of Commerce degree.

Suriya is married to actress Jyothika, with whom he worked with in as many as seven films. The couple, after being together for several years, got married on 11 September 2006. They have two children, a daughter named Diya (born 10 August 2007) and a son named Dev (born 7 June 2010).



Favourite Actress - keerthy Suresh


Keerthy Suresh (born 17 October 1992) is an Indian actress who appears predominantly in Tamil and Telugu films, in addition to a few Malayalam films. She won the National Film Award for Best Actress for portraying actress Savitri in the Telugu film Mahanati (2018).

Keerthy went on to star in successful films such as Ring Master (2014), Nenu Sailaja (2016), Rajinimurugan (2016), Remo (2016), Bairavaa (2017), Nenu Local (2017), Mahanati (2018), Sarkar (2018), Thaanaa Serndha Koottam (2018), Penguin (2020), Miss India (2021), Good Luck Sakhi (2022) and Sarkaru Vaari Paata (2022). Keerthy received Filmfare Award for Best Actress – Telugu nomination for Nenu Sailaja and Mahanati, winning for the latter.
